My Personal Goals

These are my 3 personal Goals that I wish to achieve:

  1. No food wastage
    75%
  2. Reduce plastic usage
    33%
  3. Reduce usage of vehicles
    50%
1. No food wastage


How far I am in to achieving it:
75%

My first goal is to have 0 food wastage as one of the world's biggest problem is world hunger. By reducing the food waste, I can help redirect surplus food to those in need through donations to food banks, local organisations that distribute food to people facing food insecurity.

2. Reduce Plastic usage


How far I am in to achieving it:
33%

Plastic has been widely used in our day to day lives and the non biodegradable plastic materials are getting in the way of our food chain. This can cause death to several animals and destory habitats. Therefore, I aim to reduce the usage of plastic and even if I did used it, I will recycle them.

3. Reduce usage of vehicles


How far I am in to achieving it:
50%

Despite cars being very efficent in getting you to your destination, it emits too much carbon dioxide. Besides, most of the places can be traveled using public transport and walking a few minutes. Therefore, I plans to reduce the amount of traveling by car as much as possible and sticks to walking, cycling and using public transport.
