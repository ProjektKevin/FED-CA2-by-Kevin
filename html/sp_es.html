About SP

Singapore Polytechnic is the oldest Polytechnics in Singapore, founded in the year 1954, it played a pioneering role in shaping technical and vocational education in Singapore. Over the years, SP has continued to uphold its legacy by providing quality education and practical training across a wide range of disciplines. As the trailblazer among polytechnics, Singapore Polytechnic has a longstanding tradition of producing skilled graduates who contribute significantly to Singapore's workforce and economic development.
